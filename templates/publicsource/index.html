School History

St. Francisco Kabiero Academy started in 1997 by the late Mr. Francisco Osoro aka "Kabiero". The school was officially opened with a Holy Mass by Fr. Lawrence Nyaanga Atambo. The main objective of the founder starting a school was to help empower the community with education, being an educationist before his retirement as a teacher.

The school helps needy students from the community, especially orphans, by offering free education.
